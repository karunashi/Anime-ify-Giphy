Anime Theme Page

Instructions

Buttons located at the very top of the page should give you the ability to populate animated images to the page. To animate them, be sure to left-click on the images!

Type into the text input area labeled "Add Anime Here!"

Press the (Green) Submit button to create your very own anime-themed button (or not)!

In order to clear the page of all existing images, press the (Red) clear button.

Also be sure to pause and resume the anime-themed video playing as you're having a blast making your mark in this anime-themed page!
